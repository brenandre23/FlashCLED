FlashCLED: Early Warning Conflict Prediction Viewer for the Central African Republic

Three-Map Data Explorer: Predictions, Temporal Features, and Static Context

Open Explorer

Problem Context

Conflict dynamics in the Central African Republic are highly local and fast-moving. The goal of this explorer is to surface conflict risk signals at H3 resolution (res 5) with clear data provenance so analysts can track spikes, validate drivers, and communicate risk quickly.

Trends we monitor: shifting hotspots, seasonal environmental stressors, market shocks, displacement signals, and news-driven sentiment. Each layer keeps its own lineage so you always know if you are looking at model outputs, upstream drivers, or static context.

What you see

  • Predictions: Conflict probabilities and expected fatalities by hex/date.
  • Temporal inputs: Time-varying drivers (conflict, markets, news, environment).
  • Static context: Distances, elevation, admin boundaries.

Data Sources Overview

21 sources harmonized to H3 resolution 5 (~10km) with 14-day temporal windows.

Environmental (7)
  • CHIRPS — daily rainfall / precipitation anomalies
  • ERA5-Land — temperature, dewpoint, soil moisture
  • MODIS NDVI — vegetation health index
  • VIIRS Nightlights — nighttime light intensity (economic proxy)
  • JRC Surface Water — water body coverage and presence
  • Copernicus DEM — elevation, slope, terrain ruggedness
Conflict & Events (3)
  • ACLED Events — battles, protests, riots, fatalities
  • ACLED NLP — 8 semantic themes + 6 conflict drivers
  • GDELT — global news tone and event counts
Socio-Political (3)
  • EPR — ethnic power relations, excluded groups
  • IOM DTM — internal displacement counts
  • FEWS NET IPC — food security phase (1–5 crisis scale)
Economic (6)
  • Global commodities:
  • Gold Price (GC=F) — mining revenue proxy
  • Oil Price (CL=F) — transport/energy costs
  • S&P 500, EUR/USD — macro stability indicators
  • Local market prices:
  • WFP Food Prices — maize, rice, sorghum, oil + shock flags
Infrastructure (5)
  • GRIP4 Roads — distance to nearest road (km)
  • HydroRIVERS — distance to river (order 3+)
  • IPIS Mining Sites — distance to diamond/gold mines
  • OSM Settlements — distance to city/capital
  • Country Boundary — distance to border (km)
Demographics (1)
  • WorldPop — 100m population density (log-transformed)
Temporal Context
  • Seasonal Features — month_sin/cos, is_dry_season flag
96 Features

43 raw + 53 transformed (lags, anomalies, shocks)

Pipeline & Guarantees

Execution Flow
  1. 1
    Ingest
    ACLED, GDELT, markets, displacement, environment to raw tables.
  2. 2
    Harmonize
    Align to H3 res 5, 14-day spine; validate H3 as BIGINT.
  3. 3
    Engineer
    Lags, decays, spatial spillover; conflict, markets, news, seasonal.
  4. 4
    Train
    Horizon models (14d/1m/3m); XGBoost/LightGBM.
  5. 5
    Forecast
    Write to car_cewp.predictions (prob, fatalities, expected).
